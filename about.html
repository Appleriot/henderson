Dr. Henderson: The Dissertation Coach LLC

Inspire, Encourage, and Support is my moto.

Linda Henderson holds an ED.D in Curriculum and Instruction from Liberty University and an M. Ed. In Teaching and Learning from Liberty University. Dr. Henderson’s dissertation was a qualitative study of PARENTS’ EXPERIENCES OF TEACHER SUPPORT RELATED TO THEIR CHILD’S INDIVIDUAL EDUCATION PLAN: A PHENOMENOLOGICAL STUDY.

Dr. Henderson has over 30 years of experience in the educational field. She is passionate about motivating others to reach higher academic achievement. Dr. Henderson believes that one can never stop learning. She, therefore, considers herself a “lifelong learner”.

Dr. Henderson is passionate about helping doctoral candidates succeed. It is her desire to coach candidates in a way that they will have less stress and frustration. As one who has been through the process, she feels she must use her gifts and support others through this journey.
